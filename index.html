Your Passport and Gateway into the Digital World

CredB balances safety and security with convenience and usability while preserving privacy and accountability, letting you can make meaningful connections within fully digital experiences with both ease of use and ease of mind

Secure storage of credentials

CredB provides a secure way to store all of your credentials, including wallets, passports, and other important documents

Decentralized identity management

CredB allows you to control the keys that manage your decentralized identifiers (DIDs), giving you greater control over your digital identity

Convenience

CredB is designed to be a one-stop-shop for managing all your credentials, which can simplify the management of important documents

Portability

CredB is designed to be portable, allowing you to take your credentials with you wherever you go

Privacy

CredB is designed with privacy in mind, ensuring that your personal information is kept secure and private

Trustworthiness

CredB is designed to be a trusted solution for managing your digital identity and credentials, giving you peace of mind that your important documents are safe and secure

The last personal profile you will ever build

CredB simplifies the process of managing your personal data and reduces the risk of identity theft and online fraud. By storing all of your credentials in one place, CredB provides a trusted solution for managing your digital identity.

  • Collect verifiable credentials from original sources.
  • Create verifiable credentials backed by proofs.
  • Retire credentials that are no longer useful.

Whether for Web3 or whatever the flavour of the month is, CredB is both your sword and shield in the global masquerade called the world wide web: protecting your privacy while providing you with the means to establish trusted connections.

Built on the principles of Privacy-by-Design (ISO 31700), CredB protects your information by default from pre-emptive and impromptu threats by providing the means to make trusted transactions with trusted parties.
